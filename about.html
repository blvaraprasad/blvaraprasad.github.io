About Me (or whatever this is)

Hi. I'm Prasad. And if you’re reading this, it probably means you’re a person who clicked a link hoping for a professional backstory and got caught in a vortex of slightly existential self-awareness. Welcome.

I’m a water resources engineer by trade, a system builder by compulsion, and a chronic overthinker by destiny. Somewhere between code and coffee, I decided life needed an operating system. So I made one. It’s this. It’s me. Hi.

"You are not your job. You are not your social media bio. You are the space between a calendar notification and a decision you haven’t made yet."

PrasadOS isn’t a company. It’s not a startup. It’s an interface for living deliberately. And sometimes irrationally. It runs on curiosity, mild caffeine addiction, and the firm belief that a clean spreadsheet can fix a broken week.

I use AI, but I don’t worship it. I automate tasks, but not relationships. I like philosophy, but not philosophers. And I believe most career advice is just survivorship bias with better formatting.

If you’re here for credentials: yes, I can code. Yes, I build models. Yes, I’ve been paid to solve problems with math. But more importantly, I once made a financial dashboard that sorted grocery transactions by emotional damage. That should count for something.

This site is my test bench, my resume, my creative lab, and occasionally my rant platform. Proceed accordingly.
